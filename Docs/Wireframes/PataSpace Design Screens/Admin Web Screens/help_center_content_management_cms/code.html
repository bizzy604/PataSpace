Welcome to PataSpace!

Creating an account is the first step to finding your perfect home. Follow these simple steps to get started:

  1. Open the PataSpace app on your mobile device.
  2. Tap the Profile tab in the bottom navigation.
  3. Select "Sign Up / Register".
  4. Enter your mobile number to receive a secure OTP.
Note for Agents:

If you intend to list properties, you will need to complete the identity verification process after initial registration.

Need more help? Contact our support team via the chat icon.
