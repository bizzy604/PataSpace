We've heard every concern.
Here are the real answers — no spin, no fine print.
"What if the listing is fake?"
Every new listing goes through admin review before going live. GPS coordinates are embedded in the photos — you can verify the location yourself. And if you unlock a listing that turns out to be fraudulent, you get a full credit refund.
"What if the landlord rejects me?"
The outgoing tenant knows the landlord. That's part of what you're unlocking — insider knowledge about approval chances. Some landlords have preferences; the outgoing tenant can tell you before you waste time.
"I've been scammed before. Why trust this?"
Because you pay nothing until you see what you're getting. Browse first, completely free. No deposit to a stranger. No agent fee upfront. You spend one small credit — KES 80–100 — only after you've already seen the photos, location, and price.
"What if the outgoing tenant is wrong about something?"
You're getting a human perspective, not a sales pitch. Someone who lived there will tell you the truth — because they're leaving. There's no incentive to lie. And if something is genuinely misrepresented, our disputes team steps in.
